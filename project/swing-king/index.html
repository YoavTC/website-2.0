About:

Swing King is a 2D platformer game where you play as a monkey who swings through the jungle using a vine. The goal is to collect bananas while avoiding obstacles and finish the level.

It was created for the GMTK Game Jam 2025, with the theme "Loop". I worked on this project with Shay Goft and Ohad Dori helped us with some of the art. The game was developed in Unity, and I was responsible for most of the programming, and some art assets, while Shay focused on level design.
