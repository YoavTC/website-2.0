About:

Chef Shooter was my first complete project in Unity, I made it from my already existing knowledge of C#, and some YouTube tutorials.

The game is a simple 2D wave-shooter where you play as a chef who needs to protect his kitchen from incoming rats. The rats vary in size and speed, and the more you kill, the faster they spawn.
I made the visual art for the game, while a friend of mine Kuchy, made the soundtrack.
