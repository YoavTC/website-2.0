Israel Wolfenstein Game


DISCLAIMER: This game is satirical. It does not condone or promote violence or terrorism, and it does not reflect my personal views.

The Idea:

When I started working on this game, it was (and still is as of 26/06/23) a time of uncertainty in Israel. There was a big movement to change how laws are passed and regulations are approved, a move that would give more power to the government and less to the Supreme Court.

I am not going to sit here and pretend that I know how this stuff works, but the gist of it is that it will turn Israel from a democratic country to a less democratic country.

A lot of people are comparing the situation to how Nazi Germany rose to power in the early 1920s. Although I think this example is a bit extreme, it can happen, and it has happened in the past.

The comparison reminded me of the old game Wolfenstein 3D. A game where you play as an American hero that goes into Hitler's castle of evil to defeat Nazi Germany once and for all.
Cartoonish sillyness aside, I like the game very much, and consider it, like many others, the foundation of first person shooters.


Technical Description:

This game was made in the Unity game engine, I made all of the art myself, apart from a 3D model that my mother's friend Atar Brosh kindly gave me the rights to use. The music was taken from a few websites (legally, of course), and credits can be found in the pictures section down below.
