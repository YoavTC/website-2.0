My favourite and only podcast I watch is Plat Chat Valorant. It's a VALORANT Esports podcast hosted (most of the time) by VALORANT analysts or commentators. I really enjoy it (especially mimi she's the funniest) and I was always impressed by the quality of the show and its live tools as the members in the podcast choose and rank pro teams.

The only thing that stood out was the coin toss website that Kurt, the producer of the show, used to determine the better team when the 4 cast members disagreed and a tiebreaker needed to be chosen. They used a simple website that simply makes a 50/50 random decision and tells you the outcome. The website, while useful wasn't on par with the rest of the production quality, so I reached out to Kurt and asked if they needed a better-looking simulator.
